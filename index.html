About us

Thanh Xuan Media is the local Vietnamese television station headquartered in Hanoi. The station was established in 2023 and operated completely by volunteers. We offer a diverse mix of shows dedicated to being responsive to all age groups and interests. Our programming includes news, special events, documentaries, entertainment and educational content. With a powerful distribution network, our mission is to preserve, promote the richness of Vietnamese culture, and inspire you to explore the world. Thanh Xuan Media is only available via Internet, airing 4 hours every Sunday.

Highlights

  • Arts & Culture News - a glimpse into the Vietnamese culture and lifestyle.
  • Vietnamese and international movies
  • General entertainment
  • Exciting sporting events from around the world.
