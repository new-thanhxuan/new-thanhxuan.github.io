About us

Thanh Xuan Media is a Vietnamese television network headquartered in Thanh Xuan, Hanoi. Founded in 2023, Thanh Xuan Media features a weekly telecast, 14 hours on Sunday. We offer a diverse schedule of shows, which is designed to appeal to all age groups and interests, ensuring the broadest possible audience. Our programming includes arts & culture news, documentaries, entertainment and educational programs in English and Vietnamese. Viewers can enjoy the program from wherever they are.

Programming highlights

  • Arts & Culture News - Produced and delivered by our staff.
  • Vietnamese and international movies.
  • General entertainment.
  • Exciting sports events from around the world.
